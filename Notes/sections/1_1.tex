Processes and tasks are two different entities, that can be informally described as:
\begin{itemize}
    \renewcommand{\labelitemi}{-}
    \item \textbf{Computational process}: what we have to do, like a Python program. 
    \item \textbf{Computational task}: what we really want to achieve.
\end{itemize}
There may be several processes for solving the same task, but sometimes the task is so hard to compute that no process can solve it. At the beginning,
both of them are dependent from the \textbf{context}, but introducing a \textbf{model} (\textit{arrow in the representation}) we are able to make them
totally independent.
